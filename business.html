Big Data and Business Intelligence

Big data is a term that describes the large volume of data, both structured and unstructured that covers a business on a day-to-day basis. It's not the amount of data that's important. It's what organizations do with the data that matters. Big data can be analyzed for insights that lead to better decisions and business strategies.
