What is AI?

AI is a set of technologies that allow machines and computer systems to learn, comprehend, and act simulating human intelligence.

Why Businesses should use AI?

Neural networks and Deep learning algorithms interpret data through different kinds of machine learning, labeling or clustering raw input. This helps businesses from all industries with customer segmentations, behavior prediction, engagement, retention, loyalty, big data analytics, and security.

What is the Future for AI?

As the world becomes more data-driven, AI promises disruption and productivity in data collection and analysis beyond the capabilities of mankind
